## Overview

DecideAI is a web-based application designed to help individuals make better decisions with clarity and confidence. 

Instead of providing generic advice, DecideAI guides users through a structured decision-making process and leverages Artificial Intelligence to analyze their situation, evaluate risks, identify key factors, and project potential outcomes.

The platform acts as an intelligent decision support assistant, helping users understand the implications of their choices before taking action.

---

## Key Features

### 🎯 Intelligent Decision Analysis

Generate personalized recommendations based on the user's situation and goals.

### 📊 Decision Clarity Score

Provides a confidence indicator based on the quality, consistency, and completeness of the information supplied.

### 🔍 Key Factor Identification

Highlights the most influential elements affecting the decision.

### ✅ Benefits & Risks Assessment

Evaluates potential advantages and drawbacks associated with each option.

### 🚀 Future Scenario Simulation

Projects likely outcomes for different courses of action.
